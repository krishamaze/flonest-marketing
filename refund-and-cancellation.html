Refund & Cancellation Policy

Subscription cancellation terms for FineTune SaaS

Last updated: 14-11-2025 10:00:00

Important: This policy governs subscription cancellations and refunds for FineTune SaaS. Physical product references do not apply.

1. Trial & Billing

  • The Starter tier is free and can be cancelled anytime via support request.
  • Custom or Growth subscriptions are billed in advance per the agreed billing cycle.
  • Invoices are non-transferable and payable via Cashfree or other approved methods.

2. Cancellation Requests

To cancel, email finetunetech.e@gmail.com from the registered address with subject "Account Cancellation". Include:

  • Organization name and GSTIN
  • Subscription tier
  • Requested effective date

We process cancellations within 2 business days and confirm via email.

3. Refund Eligibility

  • Monthly plans: Prorated refunds available if cancellation is requested within 7 calendar days of billing date.
  • Annual plans: Refunds available within 15 calendar days of billing date. After 15 days, unused months convert to service credits.
  • Implementation & custom development fees: Non-refundable.
  • Usage-based or overage charges: Non-refundable once incurred.

4. Refund Process & Timelines

  • Approved refunds are credited back to the original payment method within 5–7 working days.
  • In some cases, bank settlement may add an additional 2–3 business days for the funds to reflect in the customer’s account.
  • We will share reference numbers once the refund is initiated.

5. Service Suspension

Non-payment or chargebacks may lead to immediate suspension. To restore access, settle outstanding invoices and charges in full.

6. Data Retention Post Cancellation

  • Data remains accessible in read-only mode for up to 30 days after cancellation.
  • You may export data during this window via dashboard or support request.
  • Backups are purged within 90 days unless legally required to retain.

7. Policy Exceptions

  • We may offer service credits instead of refunds when mutually agreed.
  • Government or regulatory mandates may override this policy.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this policy to reflect product or regulatory changes. Material updates will be announced via email or dashboard notifications.
