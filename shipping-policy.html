Shipping / Service Delivery Policy

How Flonest delivers access to the SaaS platform

Last updated: 14-11-2025 10:00:00

Important: FineTune is a SaaS product. We do not ship physical goods. All services are delivered online through secure access to our web application.

1. No Physical Shipping

FineTune provides inventory management and related features as a cloud-based software service. We do not ship any physical products as part of our standard offerings. References to shipping, couriers, or logistics on other platforms do not apply to FineTune subscriptions.

2. Service Access & Onboarding Timelines

  • Account creation: Access to the FineTune web application is typically provisioned immediately after successful signup and payment confirmation.
  • Basic onboarding: Standard onboarding (account setup, basic configuration, and user access) is usually completed within 1–3 business days from the time we receive all required business details.
  • Custom implementation / enterprise onboarding: For advanced configurations, integrations, or enterprise accounts, onboarding timelines typically range from 3–10 business days, depending on scope and customer readiness.

3. Third-Party Hardware or Materials

If any third-party hardware devices or physical materials are ever required for a specific deployment or integration, we will clearly communicate such requirements, charges, and estimated timelines at the time of purchase or in the applicable order form. Unless explicitly mentioned, no physical shipments are included with your FineTune subscription.

4. Communication & Status Updates

We will keep you informed of onboarding and implementation progress via email or phone. If we anticipate delays beyond the typical timelines, our team will proactively share revised estimates and next steps.
