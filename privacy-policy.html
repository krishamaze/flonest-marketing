Privacy Policy

Transparent handling of your business data

Last updated: 14-11-2025 10:00:00

FINETUNE ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FineTune inventory management and business onboarding platform accessible at bill.finetune.store (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to operate our SaaS platform:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, organization details, GSTIN, and phone numbers provided during signup or onboarding.
  • Operational Data: Inventory records, customer contact data, invoices, product catalogs, and other business content you upload.
  • Payment Details: Billing contact data and transaction metadata processed via Cashfree or other payment partners (we do not store full card information).
  • Usage & Device Data: Log data, IP address, browser type, device identifiers, access timestamps, and application interaction telemetry.
  • Support Communications: Emails, attachments, and chat transcripts sent to our support channels.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is processed to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve SaaS features such as stock tracking, analytics, and onboarding workflows.
  • Authenticate users, authorize permissions, and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Process payments, generate invoices, and manage subscription billing.
  • Send transactional emails, product updates, and security alerts.
  • Deliver support, debugging, and incident response.
  • Comply with legal obligations, audits, and regulatory requests.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We share information only with:

  • Infrastructure Providers: Supabase (hosting, database, authentication) and Vercel (frontend hosting).
  • Payment Processors: Cashfree and other RBI-compliant gateways for subscription billing.
  • Analytics & Monitoring: Tools that help us monitor uptime and performance, limited to pseudonymized data.
  • Legal Authorities: When required to comply with applicable law or enforce our agreements.

All third parties are bound by data processing agreements and comply with industry security standards.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use first-party cookies and local storage to maintain authenticated sessions, remember preferences, and improve product experience. You can control cookie behavior via your browser settings, but disabling cookies may limit certain platform features.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription and for up to 90 days after cancellation to allow data export. Backup copies may persist for an additional 30 days. We may retain information longer when required by law or to resolve disputes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your account data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Export your business data in machine-readable formats.
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential communications.

Submit requests by emailing finetunetech.e@gmail.com. We respond within 15 business days.

7. Security

We implement ISO-aligned controls, including encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS), role-based access controls, audit logging, and periodic vulnerability assessments. Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure, so please safeguard your credentials.

8. International Transfers

Our infrastructure is primarily hosted in India. If data is transferred outside India for redundancy or service provisioning, we ensure equivalent protections and contractual safeguards.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Service is intended for business users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us for removal.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product changes or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated via email or dashboard notifications, and the "Last updated" date will be revised. Continued use of the Service after changes indicates acceptance.
